Domains

RECON SUBSEA is a team of Engineering students from various fields at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur, India. We are an Underwater Marine Robotics Student Team consisting of several Domains such as Corporate, Graphic Design, Electrical, Coding and Mechanical.

Coding Domain

We are a domain of young tech enthusiasts who brainstorm every single idea in order to provide functionality to our ROV. It acts as a bridge between the structure prepared by the mechanical domain and the circuits provided by the electrical domain. We look over the tasks of image processing, functioning of the controllers and the manipulators. We prepare computer vision module for the live feeds. The functioning of the cameras and the feed is also managed by the coding domain. We also prepare the GUI interface for different functions. Basically, Coding domain analyses the problem statements and the tasks, divide them into subdomains and works towards making the ROV effortlessly functional and responsive to a given task-oriented situation.

Electronics Domain

In any ROV, the electronic systems frequently run in tandem with the hydraulic, mechanical, and control segments of a system. It is the brain of the ROV. It controls everything. Sensors, electric motors, power sources, etc. are crucial parts of an ROV. When, control system has to manage the vehicle while operating through a much simpler and more intuitive interface in order to operate the vehicle. A basic control of direction and proportional scaling of electrical motors is necessary to finely control the motion of the ROV. It is evident that all the electronic systems are necessary for the smooth running of an ROV.

Corporate Domain

Corporate domain acts as the backbone of the team. We work on the non-technical aspects of the team. As per required by the MATE competition, we prepare the technical documentation of the ROV. It is a concise manual on the structure and functioning of the ROV. Apart from that we maintain the team budget and finances. We also conduct outreach programs in order to help the needy, volunteer in shelter homes because we as a team believe in giving back to the society. Sponsorship and marketing are a very integral part of the coding domain because we as a student-based team require funds to design the best version of our ROV. Till date we have been very successful in collaborating with new sponsors and increasing the strength of our RECON SUBSEA family.

Mechanical Domain

The mechanical domain plays an essential role in the team since it is responsible for designing the frame of the ROV and also works in areas related to thruster configuration, buoyancy and ballast, waterproofing and control station. The domain consists of a group of diligent people with an expertise in their field of work who exhibit team spirit .They are committed to the mission of the team and live by the values that have been created by the culture of the team which promotes unity, sportsmanship, harmony and a motto to always keep moving forward. The domain works in coordination with other domains and has played a huge role in leading the team to where it is today.

Graphic Designing

The graphics Domain is responsible for various tasks such as- 1. Designing and mentaining the social media handles of the ReconSubsea. 2.Designing the I'd cards and team t-shirts 3. Constructing personalised cover pages and templates for the Tech Report that is to be submitted for The MATE ROV competition. The team comprises of students from 1st, 2nd years and heads from 3rd year who work diligently and passionately for the team.
